# Design System

## Overview

Souqna presents itself as a calm bilingual commerce workspace for GCC home businesses. The site alternates between a dark charcoal editorial surface and a warm cream grid, with large display typography, thin divider lines, and quiet product detail instead of loud SaaS chrome. Arabic and English appear as equal voices throughout the page. The visual personality is restrained, founder-friendly, local to Doha, and operationally focused.

## Colors

- **Ink Surface**: `#0A0A0A` - hero background and deepest product moments.
- **Charcoal Surface**: `#2A2A2A` - main dark panels, pricing highlight, footer.
- **Soft Ink**: `#1F1B16` - deep warm text and control accents.
- **Cream Surface**: `#E8DCC4` - primary light workspace surface and marquee bed.
- **Pale Content**: `#F7F7F3` - bright text on dark surfaces.
- **Quiet Border**: `#D1C7B2` - grid lines, plan separators, muted UI structure.
- **Card Neutral**: `#E7E6E0` - nav and pale UI containers.
- **Mid Neutral**: `#353533` - logo chip backgrounds and secondary dark blocks.
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - small high-contrast highlights.
- **Black**: `#000000` - halftone depth and deep contrast.
- **Do not use**: orange accents such as `#ffbe8a`, neon purple, or generic blue SaaS gradients.

## Typography

- **English Sans**: `Exo 2` / captured `__exo2_95a469`, weights 100-900. Used for large rounded display headlines, body text, pricing, and interface labels.
- **Arabic Display**: captured `__thmanyahSerifDisplay_191a77`, weights 300-900. Used for Arabic brand and bilingual supporting lines.
- **Arabic Text**: captured `__thmanyahSerifText_ed2411`, weights 300-900. Used for smaller Arabic copy.
- **Mono**: captured `__jetBrainsMono_57b628`, weights 400-500. Used for labels like "MADE IN DOHA FOR GCC", section markers, day labels, and terminal-like product metadata.
- Heading scale is oversized and airy: hero around 104px, section headings around 72px, body around 18-20px, labels in tight uppercase mono.

## Elevation

Depth comes from flat color shifts, thin borders, grid lines, soft blur behind the floating nav, and dense halftone texture rather than conventional shadows. Cards and pricing columns are framed by 1px dividers with subtle rounded corners. The logo/app ecosystem is presented as pill chips on a cream band, creating tactile depth without glossy effects.

## Components

- **Halftone Hero Field**: dark full-bleed field with a soft dot matrix that creates depth behind the main promise.
- **Floating Capsule Nav**: centered rounded nav with pale translucent surface, brand lockup, locale toggle, and small icon controls.
- **App Logo Marquee**: two rows of rounded dark chips carrying marketplace, payment, analytics, and social icons.
- **Bilingual Onboarding Grid**: four question rows with English labels, Arabic small text, and quiet dividers.
- **Process Ledger**: dark timeline rows for Open, Write, Operate, Improve, with Roman numerals and mono operational labels.
- **Unified Surface Section**: large statement with three proof columns about code/copy/contracts, GCC payments, and post-launch support.
- **Placeholder Metric Cards**: three black panels on cream awaiting real founder numbers.
- **Pricing Table**: four-column plan grid, with Pro+ highlighted in charcoal and QR prices as the visual anchor: Free, Pro QR 49, Pro+ QR 145, Max+ QR 235.
- **Footer Wordmark**: oversized outlined Souqna letters sitting behind practical footer links.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's

- Use exact black, charcoal, and cream surfaces from the captured palette.
- Keep layouts editorial and grid-led, with thin dividers and generous whitespace.
- Treat Arabic and English as equal visual voices.
- Use mono labels sparingly for operational structure.
- Let the logo and app/payment chips carry the product ecosystem story.

### Don'ts

- Do not introduce bright neon gradients or generic blue SaaS color.
- Do not use glossy device mockups that fight the calm workspace identity.
- Do not overcrowd the page with stock photography.
- Do not round cards beyond the site's restrained capsule/table language.
- Do not replace the tactile cream grid with plain white.
